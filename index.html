Deletion Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2026

1. Account Deletion Overview

Users of Codino may request deletion of their account at any time. Account deletion permanently removes your access to the platform and deletes personal information associated with your account, subject to certain legal exceptions.

2. How to Request Deletion

To initiate a permanent account deletion request, please send a written request to our Privacy Team:

Email: madmaxsecondac@gmail.com

Please include the following in your request:

  • Full name associated with the account
  • Registered email address
  • A clear statement requesting permanent deletion

3. Data Retention

Most personal data is removed immediately. However, certain information may be retained for:

  • Legal, regulatory, or tax obligations
  • Security and auditing purposes
  • Fraud prevention

Retained data is stored securely and is never used for marketing.

4. Irreversible Action

Account deletion is permanent. Once completed:

  • All learning progress and XP are lost
  • Earned certificates are invalidated
  • Community posts and history are removed

5. Response Time

Codino will confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days and complete the deletion process within 30 calendar days.
