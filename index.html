User Account
Deletion Protocol

This document delineates the formal procedures, rights, and institutional obligations regarding the permanent termination of user data and accounts within the Codino ecosystem.

Document ID: CDA-2026-P01 Last Revised: January 28, 2026
01

Account Deletion Overview

Users of the Codino platform ("Account Holders") may exercise their right to request a permanent cessation of services and the absolute deletion of their account at any given time. This process signifies a terminal action that permanently removes access to the Codino infrastructure and initiates the systematic purging of all personal identifiers associated with said account, subject to the regulatory exceptions outlined in Section 04.

02

Deletion Procedures

I. In-App Direct Deletion (Recommended)

For the most efficient processing, users should utilize the automated deletion protocols within the application:

1 Open the Codino App
2 Navigate to the Profile section
3 Access the Settings menu
4 Execute Delete Account for comprehensive data and account eradication

03

Non-Reversible Action

Account termination constitutes a final, irreversible operation. Upon the execution of a deletion request:

  • All pedagogical progress, XP accumulations, and curriculum milestones are permanently voided.
  • Proprietary service data, user configurations, and interface preferences are extracted and purged.
  • Access to any accumulated balance, usage history, or premium entitlements is immediately forfeited without possibility of restoration.
04

Data Retention & Exceptions

While account deletion initiates the removal of the majority of personal data, Codino reserves the right to retain specific datasets to satisfy the following obligations:

  • Compliance with governing legal, regulatory, or fiscal statutes.
  • Mitigation of fraudulent activities and resolution of active disputes.
  • Maintenance of system security integrity and comprehensive auditing procedures.

Retained data is isolated within secure, non-operational environments and is strictly excluded from marketing or profiling activities.

05

Administrative Timelines

Upon receipt of a verified and authenticated deletion request, Codino undertakes to:

  • Acknowledge and confirm receipt within three (3) business days.
  • Finalize the comprehensive deletion sequence within thirty (30) calendar days, unless extended timelines are necessitated by complex legal or regulatory mandates.
