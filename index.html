User Account
Deletion Policy

This document outlines the procedures, rights, and obligations regarding the permanent deletion of user accounts and data within the Codino app.

Last Revised: January 28, 2026
01

Account Deletion Overview

Users of the Codino app may request to permanently close their account at any time. This process is a final action that removes your access to the platform and deletes all personal information associated with your account, subject to the legal exceptions described in Section 04.

02

How to Request Deletion

I. Delete via the App (Recommended)

For the fastest results, please use the account deletion tools inside the app:

1 Open the Codino App
2 Go to the Profile section
3 Open the Settings icon
4 Click Delete Account to remove your data and the account itself

II. Written Request via Email

You may also send a formal request for deletion to our Privacy Team:

03

Permanent & Irreversible

Deleting your account is final and cannot be undone. Once processed:

  • All learning progress, XP, and achievements will be lost.
  • Your settings, preferences, and account history will be permanently removed.
  • You will lose access to any remaining balance or premium features.
04

Data Retention & Exceptions

While we delete most data immediately, Codino may retain some information when necessary for:

  • Meeting legal, tax, or regulatory requirements.
  • Preventing fraud or resolving disputes.
  • Maintaining system security and auditing.

Retained data is stored securely and is never used for marketing purposes.

05

Processing Times

Once we receive your deletion request, we will:

  • Confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days.
  • Complete the full deletion process within 14 calendar days.
